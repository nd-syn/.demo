Our Story

Three Decades of Curiosity. One Workshop That Cares.

IT Life Solution was founded in 2010 with a simple promise: treat every customer's PC like it's our own. Fourteen years later, that promise still drives everything we do.

Who We Are

Our business has grown almost entirely through word-of-mouth — because we believe the best advertisement is a customer who walks out happy and tells their friend.

Frequently Asked

Quick Answers

Do you sell only branded PCs, or can I get a custom build?
Both! We sell branded desktops and laptops from Dell, HP, ASUS, Lenovo and MSI, and we also design completely custom builds tailored to your budget and use-case. Most customers go the custom route — better performance per rupee.
How long does a typical PC build take?
Once parts are in stock, a standard build takes 24–48 hours including assembly, cable management, OS installation, driver setup and a 12-hour stress test. Complex water-cooled or custom-painted builds may take 5–7 days.
Do you provide warranty on repairs?
Yes. All our repairs come with a 90-day service warranty on the work performed. Replacement parts carry the manufacturer's warranty, which we register on your behalf.
Can I get on-site support for my office?
Absolutely. We offer AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) plans with scheduled visits, priority on-site support and emergency response. Perfect for offices, studios, schools and cyber cafés.
Do you ship parts outside Kolkata?
Yes — we ship genuine components pan-India with insured courier. Custom builds are typically built locally in our workshop, but we can arrange custom builds for outstation customers with detailed video documentation.
