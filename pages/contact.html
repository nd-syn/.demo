Quick Answers Before You Visit

Do I need an appointment to visit the showroom?
No appointment needed. Walk in any time during business hours. If you want a dedicated consultation for a custom build, we'd recommend calling ahead so we can have the right technician ready.
How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?
Phone calls and WhatsApp messages are usually answered immediately during business hours. Form submissions and emails get a response within 1 hour during the day, and by next morning if sent overnight.
Do you offer pick-up and drop for repairs?
Yes, within Kolkata municipal area for a small fee depending on distance. AMC customers get complimentary pick-up and drop.
Can I pay by card / UPI?
Absolutely. We accept cash, all major debit/credit cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfer. GST invoice provided for all transactions.
